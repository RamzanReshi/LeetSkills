Our Contribution

How LeetSkills builds real, lasting skills

The Problem With Existing Platforms

"You can solve 500 LeetCode problems and still struggle in your first real engineering job — because the skill that matters is thinking through ambiguity, not memorizing a binary tree traversal."
⚠️
Pattern Memorization

Platforms reward memorizing solutions, not building thinking muscle

⚠️
Binary Feedback

Pass/fail tells you what — not why or how to grow

⚠️
Narrow Skill Scope

Only coding — ignores communication, judgment, teamwork

The AI Shortcut Trap

When users can paste a problem into ChatGPT and get a solution instantly,
the exercise becomes copy-paste, not learning.

❌ The Shortcut Path

1. See problem
2. Copy to AI
3. Paste solution
4. Submit & move on

Result: No reasoning developed. No skill retained.

✅ The LeetSkills Path

1. Read scenario
2. Write thinking trace first (your reasoning, ≥80 chars)
3. Then write your response
4. AI evaluates your process, not the answer

Result: Reasoning becomes the habit.

LeetSkills

How We Enforce Independent Thinking

📝
Mandatory Thinking Trace Users must write their reasoning process before their final answer. Minimum 80 characters enforced — no thinking, no submission.
⏱️
Timed Scenarios Each scenario has a time limit (10–15 min). Urgency discourages multi-tab AI consultation and encourages immediate thinking.
🔍
Process-Based Scoring The rubric rewards how you think, not whether the answer matches a model solution. Copying an AI response without reasoning scores poorly.
